About SoundFlex

SoundFlex is a smart music assistant platform designed to help users upload, analyze, and manage their music files easily in one place. The system focuses on simplicity, accuracy, and user-friendly interaction.

What SoundFlex Does

  • Allows users to create an account and securely log in
  • Supports uploading music files in MP3 and WAV formats
  • Automatically analyzes uploaded audio and identifies its genre
  • Stores user upload history for future reference
  • Ensures only valid music files are accepted
  • Provides a clean and easy-to-use interface

Why SoundFlex

SoundFlex helps musicians, students, and music enthusiasts quickly understand their audio content without complex tools. Everything is handled through a simple web interface with secure access.
